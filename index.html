About the Game

World Shattered, a Fate Unwritten
The Lands Between stand in ruin, their golden grace faded, their rulers fallen to madness and ambition. In this forsaken realm, the Elden Ring lies broken, its fragments scattered across the lands, held by demigods of immense power. As a Tarnished, guided by lost grace, you must forge your own path—through towering castles, desolate ruins, and vast open fields teeming with danger. Face legendary foes, wield arcane sorceries and mighty steel, and uncover the secrets that bind this world together. Will you rise as the Elden Lord, or succumb to the darkness that consumes all? Explore the Lands Between

Embark on a journey through an open world teeming with danger, mystery, and breathtaking beauty. From the towering castles that reach the heavens to the desolate, forgotten ruins that whisper of ancient secrets, every corner of the Lands Between holds a story waiting to be discovered. Key Features

Deep Lore - Storytelling

  • Hidden Storytelling – Told through item descriptions, environmental clues, and NPC dialogue.
  • Themes of Fate & Free Will – Gods, outer forces, and rebelling against destiny.
  • Multiple Endings – Your choices shape the world.

RPG Elements - Character Customization

  • Stats Matter – Leveling up Strength, Dexterity, Intelligence, etc. changes how you play.
  • Tons of Builds – Want to be a giant sword-wielding brute? A stealthy dagger user? A cosmic magic sorcerer? You can.
  • Armor & Fashion – Every piece of armor changes your look, and "fashion souls" is half the game

Magic - Ashes of War

  • Magic is super strong – You can use sorceries (intelligence-based) or incantations (faith-based) for powerful attacks.
  • Ashes of War – Special move sets for weapons, like fire slashes or teleportation strike
